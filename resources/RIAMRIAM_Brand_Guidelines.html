Clear Space & Sizing
Breathing Room

Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the letter "R" in the wordmark around all sides of the logo. Never place the logo smaller than 24px tall for digital or 10mm tall for print.

05
Usage Rules
Do's & Don'ts

Consistent use of the RIAMRIAM mark ensures it remains recognisable and retains its authority. Below are the essential rules for correct application.

✓ Do

Use the logo on approved backgrounds — white, parchment, sage green, and charcoal.

✗ Don't

Place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a solid colour block behind it.

✓ Do

Maintain the minimum clear space on all sides as defined in section 02.

✗ Don't

Stretch, skew, rotate, or otherwise distort the logo in any direction.

✓ Do

Use the reversed (white) version when placing the logo on dark or coloured backgrounds.

✗ Don't

Recreate the logo in different colours, apply gradients, or add drop shadows or glows.

✓ Do

Use the icon-only version at small sizes (below 40px) and in favicon/app icon contexts.

✗ Don't

Add taglines, supporting graphics, or other brand elements within the logo's clear space.
